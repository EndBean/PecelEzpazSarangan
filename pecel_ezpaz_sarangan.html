About Us

A Mobile Pecel Stall
That Stood the Test of Time

Pecel Ezpaz Sarangan is a traditional Javanese food business operating under the concept of a pecel mobil (mobile pecel stall), serving food directly from a vehicle equipped with shelves and serving utensils.

Founded in October 2009 by Ismiati, this business was built to fulfill the community's need for a nutritious, delicious, and affordable breakfast in Malang City.

Starting from a simple belief that breakfast is a daily necessity that will never run out of demand, what once only sold out by midday now consistently sells out before 10:00 AM every single morning.

Our Journey

History & Growth

October 2009
The Beginning
Ismiati launched the business after recognizing a major opportunity in the breakfast segment with little competition. She started selling in front of a shophouse that has since become the café 'Selawe' in the Lowokwaru area.
2009 – 2010
Building the Foundation
Customers were still few and food only sold out by midday. Ismiati relocated to a spot in front of SDN Lowokwaru 3, just a short distance from the original location, seeking a more strategic position. She continued to stay consistent in quality throughout this period.
2010 – Present
Growth & Loyal Following
After roughly one year of perseverance, loyal customers began to grow in numbers. Today, Pecel Ezpaz Sarangan sells out before 10:00 AM every day, with customers coming from Malang, other cities across Indonesia, and even from abroad.
